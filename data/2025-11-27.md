Abstract: We determine the ring structure of the torus-equivariant cohomology of rank-one juggling varieties with rational coefficients. By realizing these varieties as cyclic quiver Grassmannians, we construct a Knutson--Tao type basis for their equivariant cohomology. Using this basis, we give an explicit description of the ring structure in terms of generators and relations, and compute the corresponding structure constants. Finally, we show that these structure constants are integral.
